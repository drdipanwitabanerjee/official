About

A physician dedicated
to women's health

Dr. Dipanwita Banerjee is a specialist Gynaecological Oncologist at a premier Regional Cancer Centre in eastern India. She completed her MCh in Gynaecological Oncology from the All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), New Delhi — one of the country's most prestigious institutions.

She is the recipient of the prestigious John N Kapoor Travelling Fellowship, which enabled advanced training in Robotic Surgery at Roswell Park Comprehensive Cancer Centre, New York — bringing world-class surgical expertise back to her patients in Kolkata.

Research & Publications

Advancing the science
of women's cancer care

Dr. Banerjee is an active clinical researcher with over 35 publications across leading national and international medical journals. Her work spans gynaecological oncology surgery, HPV vaccination, colposcopy, and minimally invasive surgical outcomes.

Trained in Good Clinical Practice (GCP) and basic biomedical research methodology, she has served as Site Principal Investigator for the Serum Institute HPV vaccine trial — contributing directly to preventive oncology in India.
