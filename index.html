About the Oncologist

A physician committed
to women's cancer care

Dr. Dipanwita Banerjee is a specialist Gynaecological Oncologist at the Chittaranjan National Cancer Institute, Kolkata — one of eastern India's leading cancer centres. She completed her MCh from AIIMS, New Delhi, and is a recipient of the prestigious John N Kapoor Travelling Fellowship.

This fellowship enabled advanced training in Robotic Surgery at Roswell Park Comprehensive Cancer Centre, New York — bringing internationally recognised surgical expertise to her patients. She is an IFCPC IARC certified colposcopist and has served as Site Principal Investigator for the Serum Institute HPV vaccine trial.

Research & Publications

Advancing the science
of women's cancer care

Dr. Banerjee is an active clinical researcher with over 35 publications in leading national and international medical journals. Her work spans gynaecological oncology surgery, HPV vaccination, colposcopy, and minimally invasive surgical outcomes.
